Privacy Policy

Play-Store-ready privacy terms for riders, drivers, and web users.

This policy explains how Nandu Travels collects, uses, stores, and protects personal information across the Move With Nandu rider app, driver partner app, website, and related services.

Effective Date: 16 March 2026 Operator: Nandu Travels

Welcome to Move With Nandu, operated by Nandu Travels ("Company", "we", "us", or "our"). This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your personal data when you use the Move With Nandu rider app, driver partner app, website, and related services (collectively, the "Service").

By using our services, you agree to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

1. Information We Collect

A. Information You Provide

When you create an account or use the service, we may collect:

  • Full name
  • Phone number
  • Email address
  • Profile photo
  • Payment details
  • Ride booking information

Driver Partner Verification (KYC)

Driver partners must submit verification documents including:

  • Aadhaar Card
  • Driving License
  • Vehicle Registration (RC)
  • Vehicle Insurance
  • Profile photograph or selfie

These documents are used only for driver verification and legal compliance.

2. Location Information

Riders

We collect precise location data while the app is in use to show nearby drivers, calculate fares, and track active rides.

Driver Partners

Driver partners allow location access in both foreground and background to receive nearby ride requests, enable live ride tracking for riders, and improve safety and fraud prevention.

Background location is used only while the driver is online or during an active trip.

3. Automatically Collected Information

When you use our services, we may collect device type, operating system, app usage information, IP address, crash logs, and diagnostics. This helps us improve performance and security.

4. How We Use Your Information

  • Provide ride matching between riders and drivers
  • Process payments
  • Verify driver identity
  • Improve platform performance
  • Detect fraud and unsafe behavior
  • Provide customer support
  • Comply with legal requirements

5. Information Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Information may be shared only in the following situations:

Between Riders and Drivers

During a ride we may share driver name, photo, vehicle details, location, and rider pickup and drop locations.

Service Providers

We may share limited data with trusted services for cloud hosting, OTP verification, payment processing, and map and navigation services.

Legal Authorities

Information may be disclosed if required by law enforcement, court order, or government authorities.

6. Data Retention

We retain user data while accounts remain active or as required by law. Ride records and financial transactions may be stored for tax and regulatory compliance.

7. Account Deletion

Users can request deletion of their account and personal data by contacting support@movewithnandu.com. Upon verification, we will delete personal data except information required for legal compliance, fraud prevention, or dispute resolution.

8. Data Security

We use technical and organizational measures to protect data, including:

  • Encrypted connections
  • Restricted access controls
  • Secure storage of documents

However, no digital platform can guarantee absolute security.

9. Third-Party Services

Our platform may integrate services such as payment gateways, map providers, and messaging services. These providers have their own privacy policies.

10. Children's Privacy

Our services are not intended for users under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal data from minors.

11. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Updates will be published on this page and continued use of the service indicates acceptance of the revised policy.
